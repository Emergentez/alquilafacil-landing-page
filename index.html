BENEFITS OF USING ALQUILAFÁCIL

With AlquilaFácil, both hosts and users enjoy a unique and enriching experience. Here are some key advantages:

Variety of spaces adapted to each need

For landlords: Publish any type of premises, from auditoriums to outdoor terraces. Expand your visibility on a platform specialized in events.

For tenants: Explore unique spaces for social events, meetings or coworking. Access premises filtered by location, type or capacity.

Fast and secure management from wherever you are

For landlords: Manage your spaces from the web: edit prices, availability, report maintenance and view reservation history.

For tenants: Review your past reservations, add spaces to favorites and check the available calendar directly from your mobile app.

Real-time security and control with IoT sensors

For landlords: Receive alerts if smoke, overcrowding or high noise is detected. Protect your property without needing to be present.

For tenants: Receive notifications when any event rule is violated, so you can act before the landlord intervenes.

Real reviews that generate trust

For landlords: Know what tenants think of your spaces and improve your reputation with each good review.

For tenants: Check the opinions of other users before booking, making sure the space meets your expectations.
